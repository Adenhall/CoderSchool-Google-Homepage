Vung Tau - Saigoneer's Retreat

Being situated no further than 2 hours of driving away from Sai Gon, Vung Tau is the most popular on-a-whim vacation spot for Southern youths in Viet Nam. But what are the attractions? The views? The kind of foods? Let's find out!
Located in the tropical monsoon climate region of the Southeast, Vung Tau possesses a typical 2-season climate:

  • Rainy season: May - October with southwest wind
  • Sunny season: November - April and the northeast monsoon.

    Back Beach - The most visited part of Vung Tau

    Also known as Thuy Van beach, it has the length of more than 8km. This is easily one of the most beautiful beach of Viet Nam as well as the hottest spot in Vung Tau.
    Featured with lengthy beach line with scattered rocks on layers of sand, Back Beach's beauty was specially created by Mother Nature. The beach ranges from Nho mountain to Cua Lap, attracts many young visitors with big waves.

    Front Beach - The exciting beach

    If you enjoy the Back Beach in the day, you'll have an even better time experiencing the nightlife at the Front Beach in the evening. The beach sits between 2 mountains as a small bay for fishers.
    After sunset, this is the gathering spot of entertainments. Following the shoreline is a lighting system purposely made for walks and sight seeing.

    The Lighthouse - A must-visit

    The Lighthouse holds a view of all Vung Tau. Feast your eyes with majestic mountains, endless sea and houses forming a city.
    Not only that, the road to the Lighthouse is treacherous yet poetic. It fills with trees, curve roads and the smell of the distance sea.

    Quito Statue - The Majestic

    Located on Nho mountain, Quito statue stands 32 meters tall on a height of 170 meters with its arms stretched as wide as 18.3 meters. Inside the statue are 133 steps for you to climb and enjoy the view on Quito's shoulder!

Vinpearl Land - A true Pearl

Located on Hon Tre Island with clear beaches all year round, Vinpearl Land is known as a "tropical paradise". In addition to luxurious hotels, beautiful gardens, and an ideal freshwater pool, this place also attracts visitors with thrilling game parks and spectacular 4D cinemas.

Mun Island

Hon Mun has clear waters and magnificent coral ecosystems, which has been rated by the World Wildlife Fund (WWF) as the most marine biodiversity area in Vietnam. Coming to Hon Mun, tourists cannot miss activities such as: Floating bar at sea, diving to explore coral, glass-bottomed boats ...

Ninh Van Bay

About 60 km from the coastal city of Nha Trang, located on Hon Meo peninsula, Ninh Van Bay is characterized by wild and pure features. This place is especially suitable for those who want to find a relaxing feeling in the calm sea space. In addition, you can also watch coral, scuba diving or try surfing, windsurfing, kayaking.

Aquarium - Oceanographic Institute

Oceanographic Institute was established in 1923, located at No. 1, Cau Da is about 6km southeast of Nha Trang city center. It has more than 20,000 specimens of 4,000 types of marine and freshwater creatures collected and preserved for many years. In particular, to the Oceanographic Institute you will admire the giant whale skeleton nearly 26m long, 3m high with 48 fully restored vertebra.

Ponagar Tower

Ponagar Tower is a beautiful ancient architectural work of Cham people, 2km from the center of Nha Trang City to the North. The relic complex consists of four towers, two temples, the largest tower of the goddess Ponagar.
After visiting Ponagar Tower, you can combine mud bathing at Thap Ba hot spring resort nearby.
